Sabrina Carpenter has captivated audiences worldwide as a singer, songwriter, actress, and style icon. With numerous gold and platinum certifications, her music resonates both on stage and in the studio, drawing sold-out crowds globally. As a star on television and in film, she has garnered immense fandom. Signed to Island Records, Carpenter has delivered hit singles like “Skin,” “Fast Times,” and “Vicious,” with recent platinum certification for “Nonsense.” Her acclaimed fifth studio album, "emails i can’t send," received gold certification and critical acclaim, featuring the chart-topping single "Feather." Recognized on Forbes' "30 Under 30" list, Carpenter's sold-out tour spanned North America, Europe, Asia, and Brazil, and she recently supported Taylor Swift on the Era's Tour.
